Coding Contests & Hackathons

Push beyond your comfort zone with real-world coding

Tech Grandha's coding contests are designed to push students beyond their comfort zones, immersing them in real-world coding experiences. Participants work on exciting projects such as building apps, games, and websites, collaborating with peers in a dynamic, educational environment. With expert guidance, mentorship, and support, students can earn certifications and accolades as they showcase their skills through fun, skill-building challenges.

These contests are in partnership with leading institutions like IIT Kanpur Antaragni, USA Computer Olympiad (USACO), HPE, the Indian Institute of Science, IIT Roorkee, IIT Guwahati, IIT Bombay, IIT Dhanbad, and BITS Pilani, providing students with exclusive opportunities and recognition from top organizations.

  • All Levels Beginner to Advanced

  • Amazing Prizes Tech gadgets & Scholarships

  • Certificates From IITs & USACO
